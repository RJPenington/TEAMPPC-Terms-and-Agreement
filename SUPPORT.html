Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a PIN to log in?

PINs are assigned by your company administrator. If you're a new employee, ask your supervisor or the office to set up your account.

I forgot my PIN. What do I do?

Contact your company administrator to have your PIN reset. You can reach them in person, by phone, or via email.

My device is locked after too many failed attempts.

For security, the app locks after 7 failed PIN attempts for 5 hours. Wait for the lockout timer to expire, then try again with the correct PIN. If you've forgotten your PIN, contact your administrator.

How do I submit a supply request?

Navigate to the "New Request" tab, search for or select the item you need, enter the quantity and job number, then tap Submit. Your request will be sent to the shop for processing.

How do I check the status of my request?

Go to the "Requests" tab to see all your submitted requests and their current status (Pending, Approved, Fulfilled, etc.).

The app isn't loading or seems slow.

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. The app requires an active network connection to sync with the server. Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, contact support.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email rj@teamppc.com with a description of the issue or your suggestion. Include screenshots if possible.
